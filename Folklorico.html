Folklorico: The Heart of Mexican Culture.


Mexico is a country located south of the United States and is divided in various regions and states. Each of these regions and states has traditional dances that are known as folklorico, which vary in styles and cultural due to their location. Baile Folklorico is “Folkloric Dance” in spanish, and got its start in the 1960’s. These dances demonstrate folk culture and express each regions personal ideas of beauty and entertainment, and well as stories of enchantment. Here is a brief summary of these regions and, some of my favorite states to dance.

These states are mostly known as mestizo, which is a mix of Spaniard and American-Indian influences, but there are also some indigenous groups in the North. The northern border states have country-western styled dances, as a result of tejano influences. One of the more popular types of dances are polkas which feature intense speed and partner work.
